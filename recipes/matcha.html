Iced Matcha Latte

This iced matcha latte recipe is one that I created to be simple enough that anyone can follow along. It's quick, adaptable, and requires no fancy equipment (my favorite kind of recipe)!

Ingredients

Matcha latte
  • 3 tsp matcha powder
  • 16oz milk or any dairy alternative
  • 2oz water
  • Crushed or cubed ice
Vanilla simple syrup
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Begin by putting a pot or kettle of water on the stove to boil. Don't worry too much about measuring your water exactly here, just be sure you have a bit more than a cup to start.
  2. Add 3 tsp of matcha to a small cup or bowl.
  3. In a separate container add 1 cup of granulated sugar (set aside your measuring cup for later steps!) and 2 tbsp of vanilla extract.
  4. Prepare your favorite cup by adding ice and 16oz of milk, I prefer Oatly for my latte but this is absolutely interchangable with your favoite milk of choice.
  5. Once your water has come to a boil, take it off the heat and add 1 cup of water to your sugar and vanilla. Mix together until sugar completely dissolves.
  6. Next, add about 2oz of water to your matcha powder and mix thoroughly to ensure there are no lumps of powder.
  7. Add your matcha mixture to your milk as well as your vanilla syrup to taste, give your latte a good stir and enjoy!

Recipe notes:

This recipe was made to be easy and adaptable, please feel free to use more or less matcha powder and water to make your mixture! Not all matchas are created equally so adjust to your preference of tea strength and consistency.

The syrup in this recipe calls for a 1:1 ratio of sugar and water, it's easiest to measure equal parts for this step in grams but for the sake of simplicity in this recipe you want to use the same measuring cup for both your sugar and water. You can also use any other flavor in place of vanilla if you please, have fun with this!
